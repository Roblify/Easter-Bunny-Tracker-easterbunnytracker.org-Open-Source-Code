Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2026
This Policy explains what information may be processed when you use the Easter Bunny Tracker website (the “Service”).

1) Overview

The Service is designed to be used without creating an account or submitting personal profile information. We aim to minimize data collection while still providing an interactive tracking experience.

1.5) Acceptance of This Privacy Policy

By accessing or using the Easter Bunny Tracker website, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agreed to abide by this Privacy Policy. Continued use of the Service constitutes acceptance of this Policy as it applies at the time of use.


2) Information Processed Automatically

Like most websites, when you visit the Service your browser may share standard technical information as part of loading the page, such as your IP address, device/browser type, and the time of your request.

3) Third-Party Content (Maps, Libraries, and Delivery Networks)

The tracker uses third-party services to display map imagery/terrain and to load required libraries. For example, the Mapbox library may be loaded from a public content delivery network (CDN), and map imagery/terrain may be requested from mapping providers. When your browser requests these resources, those providers may receive standard request data (such as IP address and user agent) in order to deliver the content.

4) IP Address Fetching (Arrival Estimate)

The tracker may fetch and use your IP address only for the purpose of estimating how far the Easter Bunny is from you and calculating when he will most likely arrive in your area.

Your IP address is kept private and is not displayed publicly or shared with anyone else outside the website for this feature.

This estimate is approximate and may not always be accurate.

5) Cookies and Analytics

The Service does not intentionally use advertising cookies. If analytics or additional tracking technologies are added in the future, this Privacy Policy will be updated.

6) Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. We do not share your IP address with other users of the Service. Some information may be processed by third-party providers as described above to deliver maps, scripts, or optional features.

7) Data Retention

We aim to minimize data retention. Some technical logs may exist at the hosting platform or third-party provider level as part of normal operations. If location-based estimates are added, we intend for any processing to be limited to providing the feature.

8) Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When changes are made, the effective date at the top of this page will be updated.

9) Contact

For privacy questions, use the Contact section in the Help menu on the tracker page.
