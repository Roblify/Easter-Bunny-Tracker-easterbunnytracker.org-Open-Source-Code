Settings - Note: Setting changes save by default

Control background music for the Easter Bunny Tracker.

Some browsers may require you to interact with the page before music can start playing.

Enable kilometer (km/h) or mile (mph) mode in determining traveling speed.

Toggle the map style to your preferance, standard or satellite. By default, standard is the map style, but satellite is a choice for your viewing experience

Enable streamer mode to hide the "Easter Bunny will arrive at your location in about" feature from viewers. We recommend enabling this if you are re-broadcasting this tracker. This is in order to protect yourself by revoking the ability for viewers of your broadcast to determine your location through the distance fetching feature.
